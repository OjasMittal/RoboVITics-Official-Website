Vellore Institute of Technology (VIT) is a private deemed university founded by G. Viswanathan in 1984. Its mission is to improve students’ lives through excellence in education and research. The Department of Student Welfare plays an irreplaceable role in creating a lively, fun, and resourceful community for students to employ their momentous years. Every year they join hands with VIT’S Clubs and Chapters to make every student find an environment where they can learn and grow together.

We are a group of ardent tech enthusiasts with the zeal to learn, to build and a thirst to be the best. A small group of passionate techies to explore different domains of technology has now blossomed into the official Robotics club of VIT, Vellore. Our mission is to spread knowledge about robotics by organizing various free workshops and seminars that help people out in finding their technical niches and build their own projects. Our members collaborate on some incredible projects and nurture glorious teams that represent us all over the world, and have several accolades to their name. With pride, all RoboVITians try to live up to the legacy left by our predecessors.

EVENTS

EQUINOX

A jam packed 36-hour session of all the possible things a tech lover could wish for. A platform where every participant can share their ideas and creativity where they will be valued by an enthusiastic audience. All this going on while there are mentors and mini workshops with abundant resources available to take you to the next stage of your learning progress.

VORTEX360

Design is not just what it looks like and feels like. Design is how it works.’ Vortex 360 is a CAD modelling hackathon powered by AutoDesk where designers are inspired to let their creative juices flow and elevate their designs into something truly original and useful.

Machine Design

For vivid design imaginations to be modelled into reality, Fusion 360 proves to be the perfect platform to execute them. With an opportunity to interact with a great community of Fusion 360 designers and experts, participants get to pivot Computer Aided Design and modern design trends with us.

Hands On Robotics

Our annual workshop, Hands On Robotics, focuses on learning the basic skills required to design fully functional mobile Arduino Robots. We provide a platform to build compact and exhilarating robots such as Line Follower, Obstacle Avoider and many more computer-controlled bots using different kind of sensors.

RoboWars

RoboWars, the flagship event of Gravitas, is a Robot Combat Competition where participants from around the globe battle each other for the coveted RoboWars Champion Title. Witness sparks fly as metal crushes metal in the most glorious battles where these war hungry machines collide for victory.

TEAM ORCUS




Team Orcus is the combat robotics division of RoboVITics. They independently design and manufacture their bots right from scratch. Founded in 2016, Team Orcus has participated in several competitions, both at the national and international-level and has seldom returned empty-handed. It is founded on the principles of authenticity, originality, dedication and perseverance. Catalyzed by its principles, Orcus has risen to be ranked 6th worldwide among all combat robotics teams by IIT Bombay’s Techfest, just one of their many achievements. Visit teamorcus.in to get inspired and know more about Team Orcus.

KARTAVYA

We, at RoboVITics, believe it is our kartavya to give back to society and make a difference in the lives of the underprivileged. RoboVITics has collaborated with educational institutions across India to spark curiosity in young minds.

We introduce children to valuable STEM skills which helps them to build a better future for themselves. Our sessions not only involve fun activities but also focus on bringing out their untapped potential. Bringing smiles to their faces and learning from their visionary minds is an absolute delight for us :)
